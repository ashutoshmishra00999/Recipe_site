Delicious Pasta Recipe

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente.
  2. Heat the tomato sauce in a pan.
  3. Mix the cooked pasta with the sauce.
